Full description:
This application adds CIT Laurentides buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.citl.qc.ca and @CIT_Laurentides on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

CIT Laurentides serves Blainville, Bois-des-Filion, Boisbriand, Deux-Montagnes, Lorraine, Mirabel, Oka, Pointe-Calumet, Rosemère, Sainte-Anne-des-Plaines, Saint-Eustache, Sainte-Marthe-sur-le-Lac, Sainte-Thérèse, Saint-Jérôme and Saint-Joseph-du-Lac.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport.
https://www.amt.qc.ca/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-laurentides-citla-bus-android

This app is not related with the Agence Métropolitaine de Transport and CIT Laurentides.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.citl.qc.ca and Twitter
