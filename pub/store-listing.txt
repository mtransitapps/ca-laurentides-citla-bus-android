Full description:
This application adds RTM (CIT) Laurentides buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.citl.qc.ca and @exo_Laurentides on Twitter.

RTM (CIT) Laurentides serves Blainville, Bois-des-Filion, Boisbriand, Deux-Montagnes, Lorraine, Mirabel, Oka, Pointe-Calumet, Rosemère, Sainte-Anne-des-Plaines, Saint-Eustache, Sainte-Marthe-sur-le-Lac, Sainte-Thérèse, Saint-Jérôme and Saint-Joseph-du-Lac.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (AMT).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-laurentides-citla-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (AMT) and RTM (CIT) Laurentides.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.citl.qc.ca and Twitter
